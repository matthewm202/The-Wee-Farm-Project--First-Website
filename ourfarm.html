Our Farm

The Wee Farm is a family run business located between Edinburgh and Carlisle which prides itself in providing an outstanding experience for all our visitors, there is something here for everybody. We have a shop which sells fresh produce and plants grown at the farm as well as a café providing fresh home baked goods. At our beautiful farm we have a petting zoo where you can see a selection of our animals as well as a play park for the little ones. We also have a number of trails where you can take a peaceful walk and hopefully see some more wild animals!

We look forward to seeing you.

A beautiful café is located on site.

We have for sale a large selecton of fresh produce which we sell in our market.

In our shop we also have for sale fresh plants and herb plants.

Many of our animals are available to visit at our petting zoo.

We have a large playpark suitable for kids of all ages.

The farm is very large and has dedicated trails mapped out for to provide a relaxing walk.
